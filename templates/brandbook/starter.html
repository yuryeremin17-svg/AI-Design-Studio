12 — Digital Applications

Digital

Website

Website should follow the brand color palette and typography guidelines. Primary navigation uses the body font (DM Sans) at 14px. Headlines use the display font (Cormorant Garamond).

Presentations

Presentation slides should use a 16:9 ratio. Title slides: dark background with centered logo and double frame. Content slides: light background with section headings in display font.

Social Media

Profile images: logo mark on dark background.
Post templates: follow brand color palette, use approved fonts only.
